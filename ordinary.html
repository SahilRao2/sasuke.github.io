who is sasuke uchiha

Sasuke Uchiha (Japanese: うちは サスケ, Hepburn: Uchiha Sasuke) (/ˈsɑːskeɪ/) is a fictional character in the Naruto manga and anime franchise created by Masashi Kishimoto. Sasuke belongs to the Uchiha clan, a notorious ninja family, and one of the most powerful, allied< with Konohagakure (木ノ葉隠れの里, English version: "Hidden Leaf Village"). Most of its members were massacred by Sasuke's older brother, Itachi Uchiha, before the series began, leaving Sasuke one of the few living.

Sasuke Kekkei Genkai


Kekkei Genkai means Bloodline limits or Unique abilities which is specific for a certain clan or a set of people. Comming to Uchiha Sasuke part , his Kekkei Genkai is a Sharingan . However there are more stages/transformation added to his Sharingan.


sharingan As soon as an Uchiha awakens the Sharingan , his abilities of Sharingan will be in a basic state and consists of One tomoe. However the Basic Sharingan has the ability to see chakra flow ( not so precise information like byakugan ) and can also see clearly if the opponent has speed in his side.


Mangekyo Sharingan: An evolved form of Basic Sharingan. Mangekyo is obtained when an Uchiha is in a trauma of losing the closest ones.


Eternal Mangekyo Sharingan: 3rd stage of Sharingan is EMS. When a person is transplanted with Mangekyo of an individual with a Bloodline or a close ties , it gives rise to EMS . If the transplanted Mangekyo is from an individual in a Bloodline , it is seen that the Quality and powers will be supported very strongly.


Rinne-sharingan: The final evolved form of Sharingan is Rinnegan . Haguromo is the only one to evolve his EMS into Rinnegan. However Sasuke got it by receiving half the powers of Haguromo in THE SHINOBI WAR .
